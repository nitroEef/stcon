Food Hygiene and Safety Checks

Food hygiene and safety checks are the core of our business. We supply the professional services needed to ensure the food safety of your establishment by detecting and identifying food hazards (physical, chemical, and biological) and providing preventive and control measures. Our analyses are performed by ISO 17025 and IPAN-accredited laboratories.

Education Services: We train employees on food microbiology and food safety management to promote safe handling and storage practices.
Food Analysis: We conduct microbial and physicochemical analyses using ISO 17025 and IPAN-accredited labs.
Environmental Monitoring: We assess and control contamination in food production processes.
Swab Analysis: We use swabs for microbial surface monitoring on hands, work surfaces, and equipment.
Food Safety Inspection: We conduct third-party inspections to verify regulatory and industrial compliance with trained food safety personnel.

Water Quality Analysis and Control

We test water samples to determine contaminants and impurities, ensuring safety for human use,aquatic life, and agriculture. We perform physicochemical and bacteriological analyses using ISO 17025 and IPAN-accredited labs.

Legionella Analysis

Legionella bacteria can cause Legionnaires’ disease and Pontiac Fever. We help identify, analyze, and control potential areas of infestation, especially in systems that aerosolize water.

Microbial Quality of Indoor Air

We evaluate airborne microorganisms in indoor environments to control pathogens. Our scientists provide detailed reports and recommendations.

Supply and Installation of Laboratory Equipment

We supply lab equipment, chemicals, glassware, and consumables for research and schools. We offer expert advice, quality products, installation, and end-user training.
